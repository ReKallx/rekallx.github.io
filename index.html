Welcome to ReKall

ReKall provides a safe place to store your life's puzzle pieces and gives access to the people that you trust the most.
ReKall provides a secure platform for a person to store their critial Life Information that can then be accessed by their Life Managers in the event of a sudden emergency, even if the ReKall user is deemed incapacitated. Rather than leaving your loved ones and co-workers scrambling in your absense, ReKall allows you to confidently address your immediate needs and private wishes in a quick, safe, and secure manner. Your life matters, and the security of your Life's Information is our upmost priority.

Life is full of uncertainty. Make sure that yours is secure when you need it most.
